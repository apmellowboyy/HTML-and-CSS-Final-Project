Resident Evil 4 is a survival horror game released in 2005 by Capcom. Even with a massive 4 on the cover, it is the 12th game and is regarded as the best in the franchise. Main character Leon Scott Kennedy is tasked to save the presidents daughter Ashley Graham, who has been kidnapped by Jack Krauser for the Los Iluminados. ("The Enlightened Ones") A religious cult led by main villian Osmund Saddler. Throughout the game you meet, defeat, and retreat from various beloved characters and enemies in memorable locations. Resident Evil 4 was able to innovate game design, taking the franchise out of 'tank controls' and introducing a third person perspective. This perspective would be replicated in other franchises, one including Dead Space. Ben Wanat and Wright Bagwell, two developers of Dead Space stated in an interview, "It's pretty obvious when you play Dead Space, to look at it and go, Yeah, it's almost like they decided to make Resident Evil 4 in space, which is exactly what we were doing." The influence of this masterpiece has never been overlooked, and was improved upon in the 2023 remake. In my opinion the remake overexceed expectations, fleshing out characters stories and having better connections between said characters. 20 years of visual upgrades and better gunplay later you got yourself endless hours of fun.
